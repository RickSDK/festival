Review Process

People can begin submitting films on Oct 1, 2020. Our goal is to make SFF the best film festival experience for people who submit films. We will achieve that by:

  1. Great venue with lots of films shown and awards given out.
  2. Real-time web page updates as film moves through the process.
  3. Guaranteed feedback with every film submitted.
  4. Free entry into our film database.
