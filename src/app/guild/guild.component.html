Review Process

Visit Film Freeway to conduct reviews.

Tier 1: All Guild Members can watch as many or as few of the films as you have time for.

To conduct a review follow these steps:

  1. Log into Film Freeway.
  2. Click "Submissions" at the top.
  3. Click on a film. ( = Films, = Scripts)
  4. Watch the film (password is directly beneath the film).
  5. Scroll down and click the blue "Rate Project" button.
Judging Form
  1. Acting, (1-10)
  2. Directing, (1-10)
  3. Writing/Story, (1-10)
  4. Sound / Music, (1-10)
  5. Stunts / Effects / Artistic Appeal, (1-10)
  • Comments to the film maker.

The scores from all the scorecards will be tabulated and the top 30 films make it into the festival.

Tier 2: Starting July 1st: The 8 guild members who have watched the most films automatically become Tier 2 members. This group will pick the top 3 movies for each award.

Tier 3: Starting July 15th: Each award will be assigned to a group of 3 "experts" who will vote on a winner from the 3 selected films.
