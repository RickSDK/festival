Review Process

Starting October 1st, you can visit Film Freeway to conduct reviews.

Tier 1: Guild Members can watch as many or as few of the films as you have time to watch and rate them in 5 categories, plus give feedback.

The judging forms look like this:

Judging Form
  1. Acting, rate from 1-10
  2. Directing, rate from 1-10
  3. Writing/Story, rate from 1-10
  4. Sound / Music, rate from 1-10
  5. Stunts / Effects / Artistic Appeal, rate from 1-10
  • Comments to other judges
  • Comments to the film maker

The scores from all the scorecards will be tabulated and the top 40 films move on to tier 2.
