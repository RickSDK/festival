2022 Snohomish Film Festival

Welcome to Snohomish FIlm Festival. Our goal is to promote local short films and all of the talented people who help make them possible.

  • 30 films selected
  • 14 Physical Trophys
  • Live festival & awards show
  • Written feedback given to all submitted films!
Requirements
  • Anyone can submit
  • Any film genre welcome
  • Any Creation date
  • Films less than 25 minutes
Submissions Closed!

Thank you to everyone who submitted a project! We ended up getting almost 60 projects submitted and made some very tough choices to select our 30 picks for the 2022 festival. All submitters should have been notified by now.

We are now choosing winners for each of the awards. You will be notified soon about these, as well as the full schedule for our festival this year. We look forward to meeting everyone in September.
