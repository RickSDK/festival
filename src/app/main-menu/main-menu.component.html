2022 Snohomish Film Festival

Welcome to Snohomish FIlm Festival. Our goal is to promote local short films and all of the talented people who help make them possible.

  • 30 films selected
  • 14 Physical Trophys
  • Live festival & awards show
  • Written feedback given to all submitted films!
 
Requirements
  • Anyone can submit
  • Any film genre welcome
  • Any Creation date
