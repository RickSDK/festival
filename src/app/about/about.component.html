About Us

Snohomish Film Festival is Washington state's newest event and forum for filmmakers from around the Puget Sound and beyond, to tell their stories through the medium of film. It aims to promote local talent as well as inspire, build and encourage current and future filmmakers, writers, and artists to showcase their work and receive regognition for their achievements. Snohomish Film Festival aims to build and foster community connections and help build a stronger film community in Washington State.

This will be an in-person film festival Saturday September 18, 2021 at Everett Performaing Arts Center from 3pm - 8pm. We hope to see all of you there.

Short films of any kind can be submitted starting October 1st, 2020. We accept films of any genre and guarantee to give the film makers written feedback from a number of local film insiders.

This festival will feature 30 films, a script reading room, film maker Q&A sessions plus a live awards show handing out 18 awards! Get your film or script submitted to be part of the fun.
